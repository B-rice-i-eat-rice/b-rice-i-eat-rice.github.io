AI History

1950 – Alan Turing proposes the Turing Test to see if machines can think like humans.
1956 – The term Artificial Intelligence is officially coined at the Dartmouth Workshop.
1970s – Early AI programs are developed to solve maths problems and play simple games.
1980s – Expert systems become popular, helping businesses make decisions.
1997 – Deep Blue defeats world chess champion
2011 – IBM's Watson wins the quiz show Jeopardy
2016 – AlphaGo defeats top chinses game Go player Lee Sedol.
2022 – OpenAI releases ChatGPT, bringing AI to million people.
2023–Present – Generative AI creates text images music videos, and helps with coding.
